💡 Loan Program Tip of the Week
FHA

First-time buyers and those with moderate credit scores (580+). FHA loans are a great fit for buyers who don't have 20% down but want to get into the market at today's rates. Popular choice for coastal condos and townhomes in the $250K–$450K range.

✓  Down payment as low as 3.5% with a 580+ credit score
✓  More flexible underwriting — allows for higher debt-to-income ratios
✓  Seller can contribute up to 6% toward closing costs
⚠ Watch out: FHA loans require mortgage insurance (MIP) for the life of the loan if you put less than 10% down. Once you build equity, refinancing into a conventional loan can remove that cost.
